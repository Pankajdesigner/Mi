Rahul Bist

CEO and Founder

Rahul is a graduate in Computer Application and an MBA from Indian Institute of Forest Management (IIFM). He has 17 years of experience in microfinance, MSME Finance, trainings and monitoring and evaluations. Rahul has experience of working in 15 countries of Asia, Europe and Africa and has advised several banks, multilateral and bilateral funding agencies and MFIs. Rahul is a co-founder and partner at M2i and heads business lines for Monitoring and evaluations and trainings.

Rahul Bist

CEO and Founder

An MBA from IRMA, Atul has 16 years of experience in microfinance, banking and market research. Atul is an expert in Financial Management and Multivariate Statistical Modeling. Statistical and financial analysis have been his area of expertise. Atul has worked in six countries of Asia and Africa. He has conducted workshops on microfinance and multivariate analysis for national and international corporates. As co-founder and partner at M2i, Atul leads development of new products and clients. He has played a leading role in developing M2i’s assessment frameworks and tools.

Deepak Alok

CEO and Founder

An MBA from the Institute of Rural Management, Anand (IRMA), Deepak also holds a Diploma in Business Finance (DBF) from the Institute of Chartered Financial Analysts of India (ICFAI). Deepak has an excellent academic record and has been Eklavya and SRTT Scholar at IRMA. Deepak has 16 years of experience in Risk Management, Microfinance, MSME Finance and project management. He has experience of working in 10 countries of Asia and Africa. He is the co-founder of the company and as a Partner he leads the development of product lines and oversees the delivery of major assignments.

Rajeev Kumar

CEO and Founder

Rajeev has 16 years of experience of in Microfinance livelihoods and MSMEs. An MBA from IRMA, He has worked with the World Bank sponsored microfinance and microenterprise project – DPIP – in Madhya Pradesh and was responsible for major credit decisions as well as capacity building initiatives of the project. Rajeev is a partner at M2i and supports in delivery of major consulting assignments.
